About NelAqua

NelAqua is a premium flavoured water brand that offers a refreshing and healthy alternative to traditional sugary drinks. Our water is sourced from natural springs and infused with real fruit extracts to provide a delicious and satisfying taste.

Our mission is to promote a healthier lifestyle by encouraging people to choose water over sugary drinks. We believe that drinking water should not only be healthy but also enjoyable. That's why we offer a wide variety of flavours to suit every taste.
